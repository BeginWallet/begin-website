# Receive Solana (SOL)

Receiving **SOL** in Begin Wallet is quick and simple. Whether you are moving funds from an exchange, receiving a payment from a friend, or withdrawing from a dApp, Jamie will walk you through the steps.

---

## How to Receive SOL

1. **Open Begin Wallet**
   - Launch the **Begin Wallet** app and make sure you are logged in.

2. **Select your Solana wallet**
   - Tap the SOL icon from your wallet home screen to open your Solana wallet.

3. **Tap "Receive"**
   - You will see your Solana receiving address along with a QR code.

4. **Copy or share your address**
   - Copy the address to your clipboard and send it to whoever is paying you, or let them scan the QR code directly.

5. **Wait for confirmation**
   - Solana transactions confirm in seconds thanks to the network's ~400ms block times. Your balance will update almost immediately.

6. **Check your balance**
   - Once confirmed, the SOL will appear in your available balance on the wallet home screen.

---

## Jamie's Tips for Secure Receiving

- **Verify the address**: Always double-check the address you share. Solana addresses are base58-encoded strings that typically start with a number or letter.
- **Use QR codes**: Scanning a QR code eliminates the risk of copy-paste errors.
- **No memo required**: Unlike some exchanges, receiving SOL in a self-custodial wallet like Begin does not require a memo or tag.

---

## Jamie's Notes

- Solana addresses are derived from your wallet's keypair and are compatible with all standard Solana wallets and exchanges.
- Receiving SOL is free -- you only pay fees when sending transactions.
- You can also receive **SPL tokens** (Solana's token standard) at the same address, though your wallet may need a small amount of SOL to create the associated token account.
